Paint flashing and layers to see the number count Even with the transform property we still have paint flashing in the beginning and at the end. Do you think the browser needs to paint it again? How to remove that? Its actually because the navigation is part of same layer as the rest of the document. Lets make it a new layer by using will-change property. This a good solution where you see paint issue for performance of a website. But be careful of this. You should not increase layers to more than required as browser will need to do work on layer management and compositing layers. There is a trade off between composite layer and paint. Also remember, that composite layers will be triggered always for any animation.
